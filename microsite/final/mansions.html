Sinan Mansions
思南公馆

Sinan Mansions are not one building, but buildings of 51 historic garden houses.
They also bring together a variety of architectural styles. There are boutique hotels, apartment hotels and enterprise mansions. Moreover, there are a variety of restaurants,
bars, coffee houses, tea houses, as well as a large number of clubs, convention centers, showrooms, cultural salons, designer shops and recreational parks. Whether you are going shopping, enjoying delicious food, staying overnight, or appreciating European style architecture, Sinan Mansions can cater to your needs.
