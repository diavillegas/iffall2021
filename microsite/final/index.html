Sinan Road, Shanghai 思南路

Sinan Road is one of the
best old streets in Shanghai
to escape from the bustling
city, with western style villas,
small delicate shops and
restaurants, in the shade
of tall plane trees.

Built in 1914 and named
‘Rue Massenet’ by the French
Concession authorities to
commemorate the famous
French musician who died
on August 13, 1912.
